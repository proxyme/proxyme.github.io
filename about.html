About

Proxy is an open source website where you can view a live stream of someones life via though a head set or smart phone or any portable/mobile camera and also tell them what to do though a chat system. The Proxys are given live feedback from the online community as to what to do in any situation.

The point of this website is to not only view this persons life but have an influence in real time on their actions and communicate to them. People can go on tours through countries they want to see and have a say in where to go and what to do. They can go to plays in New York while being in a different country.
